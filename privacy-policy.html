LapCheck Privacy Policy

LapCheck is a hardware monitoring and diagnostics application for Windows.

LapCheck reads hardware information including CPU, GPU, RAM, Battery, Storage and system information directly from the local computer.

All hardware data is processed locally on the user's device.

LapCheck does not collect, transmit, upload, sell or share hardware information with any third party.

LapCheck does not access personal documents, photos, passwords, browsing history or personal files.

The application is intended solely for hardware monitoring, diagnostics and system health reporting.
